About Us

Pastimallow Treats started from a simple passion: creating cute, sweet gifts that make people smile. What began as a hobby in our home kitchen has blossomed into a labor of love, crafting handmade marshmallow characters in soft pastel colors.

Each treat is carefully made in small batches, ensuring quality and cuteness in every bite. We believe that desserts should not only taste amazing but also bring joy the moment you see them.
